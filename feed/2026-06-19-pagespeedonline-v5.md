# New Agentic Browsing category added to PageSpeed

**Date:** 2026-06-19  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has introduced a new 'agentic-browsing' category to its Lighthouse audits, signaling a shift toward measuring site performance for AI agents.

## Details

The Categories schema now includes an `agentic-browsing` property, which references the `LighthouseCategoryV5` type. This new category is designed to group audits related to how well a page handles automated or 'agentic' browsing sessions, likely reflecting the growing importance of LLM-based crawlers and AI agents in the web ecosystem.
