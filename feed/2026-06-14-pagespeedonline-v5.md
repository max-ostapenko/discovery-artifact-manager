# New Agentic Browsing Category in Lighthouse Audits

**Date:** 2026-06-14  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Low  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has added a new 'agentic-browsing' category to its Lighthouse audit results, targeting automated and AI-driven browsing behaviors.

## Details

The Categories schema now includes a property for 'agentic-browsing' (referencing LighthouseCategoryV5). This addition indicates that Lighthouse is beginning to group and report on audits specifically relevant to how websites perform or behave when accessed by AI agents or automated browsing tools, rather than just human users.
