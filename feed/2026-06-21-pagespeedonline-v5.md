# New Agentic Browsing Lighthouse Category

**Date:** 2026-06-21

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has introduced a new 'agentic-browsing' category to its Lighthouse audit results.

## Details

The schema for Categories now includes an `agentic-browsing` property (type LighthouseCategoryV5). This addition indicates that Lighthouse is beginning to track and group audits specifically related to how autonomous agents or AI-driven browsers interact with web content, expanding the API's scope beyond traditional human-centric performance and accessibility metrics.
