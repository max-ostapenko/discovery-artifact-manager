# New Agentic Browsing Category in Lighthouse Audits

**Date:** 2026-06-17  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Low  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has added a new 'agentic-browsing' category to its Lighthouse audit results.

## Details

The API's Categories schema now includes an `agentic-browsing` field, which references the `LighthouseCategoryV5` type. This new category is designed to group audits specifically related to how AI agents and automated browsers interact with and navigate web pages, signaling a shift toward optimizing for non-human visitors.
