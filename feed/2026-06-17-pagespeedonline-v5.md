# New Agentic Browsing Audits in PageSpeed Insights

**Date:** 2026-06-17  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Low  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has introduced a new 'agentic-browsing' category to its Lighthouse reports, focusing on audits for automated or AI-driven browsing scenarios.

## Details

The 'Categories' schema now includes an 'agentic-browsing' property which references the 'LighthouseCategoryV5' schema. This addition allows developers to retrieve specific audit results related to how 'agentic' browsers—likely AI agents or advanced automation tools—interact with and perceive web pages. This signals a shift toward optimizing web content for non-human consumers.
