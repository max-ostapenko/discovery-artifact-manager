# Deprecation of Destinations get and link methods

**Date:** 2026-06-16  
**API:** `tagmanager.v2`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

Google Tag Manager has officially marked the 'get' and 'link' methods within the destinations resource as deprecated.

## Details

The discovery document now explicitly flags 'accounts.containers.destinations.get' and 'accounts.containers.destinations.link' with a deprecated status. While these methods remain functional for now, this change signals that they are slated for removal or replacement in future updates.
