# New Agentic Browsing Category in Lighthouse Reports

**Date:** 2026-06-20  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Low  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has introduced a new 'agentic-browsing' category to its Lighthouse audit results, signaling a shift toward measuring how AI agents interact with web pages.

## Details

The Categories schema now includes an 'agentic-browsing' property (type LighthouseCategoryV5). This new category is designed to aggregate audits specifically related to agentic browsing behaviors, likely providing insights into how well a page performs for automated or AI-driven user agents rather than just human visitors.
