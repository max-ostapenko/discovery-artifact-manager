# Unstructured Data Profiling and Graph-based Semantic Scans

**Date:** 2026-06-19  
**API:** `dataplex.v1`  
**Impact:** High  

## Summary

Dataplex adds support for profiling unstructured data and introduces complex graph-based semantic inference results for data scans.

## Details

This update introduces a major expansion to Dataplex's scanning capabilities. DataScan and DataScanJob resources now support a new UNSTRUCTURED_DATA_PROFILE type, accompanied by specific Spec and Result fields. Additionally, a comprehensive suite of GraphProfile schemas has been added (including NodeTypes, EdgeTypes, and ForeignKeys), enabling developers to capture and analyze semantic relationships and graph structures within their data. Finally, the LookupContextRequest now includes an all_schema_fields option to ensure full schema retrieval regardless of the defined context budget.
