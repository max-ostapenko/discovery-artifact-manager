# Vertex AI adds Veo video looping and Exa.ai grounding

**Date:** 2026-06-12  
**API:** `aiplatform.v1`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

Vertex AI introduces advanced video generation controls for Veo, including seamless looping and tessellation, alongside a new Exa.ai grounding tool and Hyperdisk support.

## Details

The video generation schema (CloudAiLargeModelsVisionGenerateVideoExperiments) now supports 'seamless' generation for temporal looping and spatial tessellation, plus the professional VIDEO_CODEC_DNXHR codec. A new 'exaAiSearch' tool allows models to ground responses using Exa.ai search results. Infrastructure-wise, PersistentDiskSpec now supports Hyperdisk types (ML, Throughput, Balanced), and documentation across several resources has been updated to reflect a shift toward 'Agent Platform' terminology.
