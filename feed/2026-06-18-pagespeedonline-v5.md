# New Agentic Browsing Category in Lighthouse Reports

**Date:** 2026-06-18  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Low  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has added an 'agentic-browsing' category to its Lighthouse audit results, targeting automated and AI-driven web interactions.

## Details

The Categories schema now includes a new property, `agentic-browsing`, which references the `LighthouseCategoryV5` type. This addition indicates that PageSpeed Insights is starting to provide specific audits and scores related to how 'agentic' browsers—likely AI agents or automated crawlers—perceive and interact with web content.
