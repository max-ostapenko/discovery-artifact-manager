# New 'Agentic Browsing' Category Added to Lighthouse Reports

**Date:** 2026-06-18  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Low  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has introduced a new 'agentic-browsing' category to its schema, enabling audits specifically for automated or AI-driven browsing scenarios.

## Details

The Categories schema now includes an `agentic-browsing` field, which references the `LighthouseCategoryV5` type. This addition suggests that Lighthouse is beginning to track and report on how well a page performs or behaves when accessed by agentic AI or automated browsers, providing a new set of audits for developers to monitor.
