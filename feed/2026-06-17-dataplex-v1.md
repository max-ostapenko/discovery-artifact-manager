# Dataplex adds Unstructured Data Profiling and Graph Profiles

**Date:** 2026-06-17  
**API:** `dataplex.v1`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

Dataplex expands its scanning capabilities with new support for unstructured data profiling and a comprehensive graph-based schema for semantic inference.

## Details

The DataScan and DataScanJob resources now support a new UNSTRUCTURED_DATA_PROFILE type, accompanied by specific Spec and Result fields for managing scans on non-tabular data. Additionally, a large suite of GraphProfile schemas has been introduced (including NodeTypes, EdgeTypes, and ForeignKeys), designed to represent complex semantic relationships discovered during scans. Finally, LookupContextRequest now supports an all_schema_fields option to prevent truncation of schema metadata in the response.
