# Dataplex adds Unstructured Data Profiling and Graph Analysis

**Date:** 2026-06-17  
**API:** `dataplex.v1`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

Dataplex expands its scanning capabilities with a new scan type for unstructured data and a comprehensive GraphProfile schema for semantic relationship inference.

## Details

The DataScan and DataScanJob resources now support a new UNSTRUCTURED_DATA_PROFILE type, accompanied by specific Spec and Result objects for managing these scans. Additionally, a large suite of GraphProfile schemas has been introduced (including NodeTypes, EdgeTypes, and ForeignKeys), enabling Dataplex to perform semantic inference and map relationships within data. Finally, the LookupContextRequest now includes an all_schema_fields option to bypass character budget limits when retrieving full schema metadata.
