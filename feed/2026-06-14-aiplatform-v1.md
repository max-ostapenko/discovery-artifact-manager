# Vertex AI adds Exa.ai grounding and Veo video looping

**Date:** 2026-06-14  
**API:** `aiplatform.v1`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

Vertex AI introduces Exa.ai search grounding, advanced video generation controls for Veo experiments, and security features for Computer Use tools.

## Details

This update brings significant enhancements to experimental video generation (Veo), including support for seamless temporal looping, spatial tessellation (horizontal/vertical), and the professional VIDEO_CODEC_DNXHR codec. A new grounding tool, ExaAiSearch, allows models to use Exa.ai for real-time information retrieval. For agentic workflows, ToolComputerUse now includes an optional prompt injection detection toggle. Additionally, PersistentDiskSpec has been expanded to support Hyperdisk types (ML, Throughput, Balanced), and Google Maps contextual widgets are officially deprecated.
