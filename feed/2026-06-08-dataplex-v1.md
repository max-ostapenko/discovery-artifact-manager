# Dataplex adds Unstructured Data Profiling and Graph Profiles

**Date:** 2026-06-08  
**API:** `dataplex.v1`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

Dataplex expands its scanning capabilities with support for unstructured data profiling and a new graph-based semantic inference model.

## Details

The DataScan and DataScanJob resources now support a new UNSTRUCTURED_DATA_PROFILE type, accompanied by unstructuredDataProfileSpec for configuration and unstructuredDataProfileResult for output. Additionally, a comprehensive suite of GraphProfile schemas has been added (including GraphProfileNodeType, GraphProfileEdgeType, and GraphProfileField), enabling the representation of complex data relationships and semantic inference. Finally, LookupContextRequest gains an all_schema_fields option to prevent truncation of schema data regardless of the context budget.
