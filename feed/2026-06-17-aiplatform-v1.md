# Vertex AI adds Veo video loops, Exa.ai search, and Hyperdisks

**Date:** 2026-06-17  
**API:** `aiplatform.v1`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

Vertex AI introduces advanced video generation controls for Veo, grounding via Exa.ai, and expanded infrastructure options including Hyperdisk support.

## Details

The video generation suite (Veo) receives significant experimental updates, including a `seamless` object for temporal looping and spatial tessellation, and support for the `VIDEO_CODEC_DNXHR` codec. Grounding capabilities are expanded with the new `exaAiSearch` tool, allowing models to query Exa.ai directly. On the infrastructure side, `PersistentDiskSpec` now supports several Hyperdisk variants (ML, Balanced, Throughput, etc.), and `ToolComputerUse` gains a prompt injection detection toggle. Note that the Google Maps widget context token is now deprecated and slated for removal.
