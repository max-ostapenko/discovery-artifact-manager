# New 'Agentic Browsing' Category Added

**Date:** 2026-06-15  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Low  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has introduced a new 'agentic-browsing' category to its Lighthouse results, targeting audits for automated agents.

## Details

The API's Categories schema now includes an `agentic-browsing` property (referencing `LighthouseCategoryV5`). This new category is designed to group audits related to how AI agents or automated browsers interact with and perceive web content, reflecting the growing importance of non-human web consumers.
