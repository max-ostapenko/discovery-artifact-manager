# Looker adds Instance Recovery and Release Channels

**Date:** 2026-06-16  
**API:** `looker.v1`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

Looker developers can now recover soft-deleted instances and manage update cadences via new release channels.

## Details

A new `undelete` method has been added to the `instances` resource, allowing for the recovery of instances deactivated due to billing issues, trial expirations, or manual requests. To support this, the `list` method now includes a `showDeleted` parameter. The `Instance` schema has also been expanded with `releaseChannel` (RAPID, REGULAR, STABLE) and `acceleratedSecurityPatchEnabled` fields, giving admins more control over their environment's update frequency and security posture.
