# Dataplex adds Unstructured Data and Graph Profiling

**Date:** 2026-06-10  
**API:** `dataplex.v1`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

Dataplex expands its data scanning capabilities with a new scan type for unstructured data and a comprehensive schema for graph-based semantic inference.

## Details

Developers can now utilize the `UNSTRUCTURED_DATA_PROFILE` enum in `DataScan` and `DataScanJob` resources, supported by new specification and result schemas (`UnstructuredDataProfileSpec` and `UnstructuredDataProfileResult`). Furthermore, a large suite of schemas for `GraphProfile` has been introduced—including `GraphProfileEdgeType`, `GraphProfileNodeType`, and `GraphProfileField`—enabling the platform to represent complex semantic relationships, foreign keys, and cardinality hints. Additionally, `LookupContextRequest` now supports an `all_schema_fields` option to prevent truncation of schema fields in the output.
