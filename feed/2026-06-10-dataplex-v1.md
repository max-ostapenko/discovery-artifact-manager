# Dataplex adds Unstructured Data Profiling and Graph Profiles

**Date:** 2026-06-10  
**API:** `dataplex.v1`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

Dataplex expands its scanning capabilities with a new scan type for unstructured data and comprehensive graph-based profiling for semantic inference.

## Details

The DataScan and DataScanJob resources now support a new UNSTRUCTURED_DATA_PROFILE enum value, accompanied by unstructuredDataProfileSpec and unstructuredDataProfileResult fields. Additionally, a large suite of GraphProfile schemas has been introduced (including EdgeType, NodeType, and Field mappings), enabling detailed semantic inference results for graph-structured data. Finally, LookupContextRequest now includes an all_schema_fields option to force the return of all schema fields regardless of the context budget.
