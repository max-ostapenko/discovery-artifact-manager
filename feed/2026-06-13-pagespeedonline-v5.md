# New 'agentic-browsing' category added to PageSpeed reports

**Date:** 2026-06-13  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Low  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has introduced a new Lighthouse category called 'agentic-browsing' to track audits related to automated or AI-driven browsing.

## Details

The Categories schema now includes an agentic-browsing property of type LighthouseCategoryV5. This new category aggregates audits specifically designed to evaluate how a page performs or behaves when accessed by agentic browsers, reflecting the growing importance of AI agents in web navigation and data extraction.
