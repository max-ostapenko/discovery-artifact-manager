# New Agentic Browsing category added to Lighthouse audits

**Date:** 2026-06-13  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Medium  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has introduced a new 'agentic-browsing' category to its audit results, targeting how automated agents interact with web pages.

## Details

The API's Categories schema now includes an `agentic-browsing` field (referencing `LighthouseCategoryV5`). This new category is designed to group audits specifically related to 'agentic' browsing behaviors, likely providing insights into how AI agents or advanced crawlers perceive and navigate a site compared to traditional human users.
