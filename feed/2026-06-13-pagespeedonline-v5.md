# New Agentic Browsing Category in Lighthouse Reports

**Date:** 2026-06-13  
**API:** `pagespeedonline.v5`  
**Impact:** Low  

## Summary

PageSpeed Insights has added a new 'agentic-browsing' category to its Lighthouse audit results, signaling a shift toward measuring how AI agents interact with web content.

## Details

The schema for Categories now includes an `agentic-browsing` property, which maps to the `LighthouseCategoryV5` type. This new category is designed to group audits specifically related to agentic browsing behaviors, allowing developers to track how well their sites perform for automated or AI-driven browsers compared to traditional human-centric metrics.
